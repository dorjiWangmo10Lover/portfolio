Project OverReview

Introducing Game Gear Hub, the ultimate destination for all your gaming equipment needs. Our app is designed to provide gamers with a seamless shopping experience, offering a wide range of high-quality gaming gear including controllers, headsets, keyboards, mice, and more. Whether you're a casual gamer or a professional eSports player, Game Gear Hub caters to your needs with top brands and the latest products. Our intuitive interface and personalized recommendations ensure that you find the perfect equipment to enhance your gaming setup. Join our community of passionate gamers and elevate your gaming experience with Game Gear Hub.

My Role:

My role involved designing and developing a seamless user experience for the website. I worked on creating an intuitive, visually appealing UI, ensuring ease of navigation, and integrating essential features like product listing, search, filtering, and a secure checkout process. Additionally, I collaborated with the backend team to implement functionality for user authentication, inventory management, and payment processing, making E-shop a responsive and user-friendly platform. My focus was on optimizing the site for performance and usability to enhance the shopping experience for our customers.

Responsibility:

My responsibility included designing a user-friendly interface, ensuring smooth navigation, and integrating key eCommerce features such as product listings, search functionality, and a secure checkout. I collaborated closely with the backend team to implement authentication, inventory tracking, and payment systems, optimizing the site for performance and user engagement. My primary focus was to create a seamless, efficient, and enjoyable shopping experience for users.

Pain Points:

1. User Experience Optimization: Balancing a visually appealing design with ease of navigation was challenging, especially ensuring that users could quickly find products, access details, and complete the checkout process without confusion or delay.

2. Integration of Secure Payment & Inventory: Coordinating secure payment gateways with real-time inventory tracking posed a challenge, as it required robust backend solutions to prevent stock mismatches and ensure secure transactions across multiple platforms.

User journey Map

Paper wireframes

The goal of wireframes was to establish the basic structure and function of each page element. By focusing on structure first, I could quickly brainstorm and iterate ideas. Creating paper wireframes was an inexpensive and efficient way to visualize these concepts, allowing me to structure the homepage effectively.

Digital wireframes

When creating digital wireframes, I based the designs on paper wireframes, peer feedback, and user research insights. This made the development process much easier.

Low-fidelity prototype

The primary user flow involves searching, selecting items, adding them to the cart, and completing payment. My prototype also allows smooth navigation through the steps.

Mockups

Usability testing revealed how real users interact with my design, helping identify weaknesses and improve the user experience. Based on the study, I refined the Login page by adjusting typefaces, colors, and icons

Take-Away

  • Responsive Design: Ensured the website is accessible and functional across various devices and screen sizes.
  • Secure Payment Integration: Highlighted the need for reliable payment gateways to enhance customer trust and security.
